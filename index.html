Sabrina Zaman

CHAIRPERSON

Sabrina Zaman has been the Chief Executive of Impact PR since 2010. She is also the official trainer for PR and media relations training that the agency offers to its corporate clients.

Ms. Zaman is a Middlesex University, London graduate, majoring in marketing communications. She has a master’s degree in International Relations from the University of Dhaka.

Tareque M Moretaza Hasan

MANAGING DIRECTOR

With over two decades of experience in journalism and 11 years in public relations consultancy, Moretaza is skilled and equipped in strategic communications, crisis management, corporate communications and damage control strategies.

He completed post-graduations in Political Science from the University of Dhaka and began his journalism career with the highest circulated and most prestigious daily Prothom Alo in 2000 and ventured into public relations consultancy field a decade later.

Journalism, consultancy and tourism are the three fronts where Moretaza has honed his expertise and bagged Monajat Uddin Memorial Award and Dhaka Reporters Unity Award for his excellences in investigative journalism.

Leadership team

Emamul Haque Shamim

DIRECTOR, CORPORATE STRATEGY

Graduated in Economics from University of Dhaka Emamul Haque Shamim has around 20 years of experience in journalism. He started his career as a journalist in 2002 with the English daily, The Independent. Thereafter, he successfully worked with state-owned Bangladesh Sangbad Sangstha (BSS) as a staff reporter.

He joined Radio Today FM 89.6 as a news editor where he played a leading role as Head of News. Mr. Shamim led the digital transformation of Radio Today. He also has experience of working with Voice of America English service. Mr. Shamim visited United States of America (USA) on an invitation of the Voice of America to cover the presidential election in 2016.

As an invited panelist, he took part in a number of international seminars on India-Bangladesh relations in India. His decorated career in journalism finally led him to the public relations industry, which he knows intimately.
